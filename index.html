Welcome to Santosh Children Home

Santosh Charitable Trust is an organization that aims to provide destitute and orphaned children with an opportunity to realize their fundamental right to a better life. The Charity aims to provide the children with an environment where they are nurtured and educated, equipping them for the future. Santosh Children's Home houses thirty five children aged between 4 and 15 years from various cultural backgrounds. In addition, the Trust is building a home for girls.

Santosh Charitable Trust operates entirely on donations from philanthropic individuals, and well wishers. The Charity is registered with the Karnataka State Government with Reg. No. 138/7-08, and all donations are tax exempt under section 80G (certificate).

Meet the children

The charitable trust is engaged in the following projects

  • Santosh Childrens Home, this is the primary activity of the Trust.
  • A girl's home for poor girls.
  • Light to the World, helping other needy children and NGOs in poor areas of Karnataka and Tamil Nadu

Child care

The children who are looked after by Santosh Chartiable Trust come from varied backgrounds, including orphans, semi-orphans, or poor parents. Contact between the parents and children is not completely broken. In some cases the children visit their parents for holidays.

Santosh Charitable Home tries to enroll as many children as possible in an English medium school, Sapthagiri Vidya Mandir, in order for prepare them for the future. 

We have a strict non-discrimination policy. We love all children, regardless of their religion, where they are from, race, colour, sex, disability, or age.

Facilities

Santosh Children's Home functions out of rented premises. These premises consist of a kitchen, an office room, and rooms for children. As you can see in the slideshow below, the living standard is very basic. There is an open area around the building where the children play. A small van has been recently purchased, thanks to donations, to enable the smaller children to travel safely to and from school.
